The Island of Whispering Sands

These are most amazing destination that can be found nowhere. The trip to the desert islands of the West Africa was one of life time memory. Off the rugged coast of West Africa, there lay a mysterious desert island known as the Island of Whispering Sands. Shaped like a crescent moon, the island was a blend of golden dunes, lush oases, and ancient ruins, all surrounded by turquoise waters teeming with life. The island was seldom visited, for it was said to be enchanted. The local tribes spoke of spirits that whispered secrets and ancient songs in the wind. Few believed the tales, but those who did never ventured near.
